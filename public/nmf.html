Description

SHP of THSEUS is a musical work for remote performers where the musical parameters are continuously changing, controlled by other performers and audience members. Based on Mythology and the thought experiment of identity, the shape of the work is predetermined but as it progresses, almost all sonic, visual, and control data is replaced multiple times over. A transformational performance overall, the visual and aural progressional will be interactive with the audience.

A. Each performer has their own digital instrument with some musical process parameters controlled by a different performer.
B. Throughout the work, each performer will be given different graphical score elements which have a prompt to interpret that graphical score through their own sonic/visual instigation or by manipulating other performers musical process.
C. The audience interface controls the musical parameters of the accompanying drone.
D. All control data for collaborative musical instrument parameters and graphical score sequencing is handled through Collab-Hub.
